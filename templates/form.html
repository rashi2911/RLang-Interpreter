R-Lang Interpreter

Interpreter for custom language named RLang

Guidelines

(1) To perform Addition use: @, Multiplication: *, Division: | , Subtraction: ~


(2)To capitalize first letter of string use: #


(3)To convert string to uppercase use: $


(4)To convert string to lowercase use: &



(5)Follows BODMAS


(6) Give string as input using: " "
